disable-youtube\" data-module=\"govspeak\">\n    \n      <p>If you have any of these 3 coronavirus (COVID-19) symptoms, even if mild, use this service to get a polymerase chain reaction (<abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr>) test as soon as possible:</p>\n\n<ul>\n  <li>a high temperature</li>\n  <li>a new, continuous cough</li>\n  <li>you’ve lost your sense of smell or taste or it’s changed</li>\n</ul>\n\n<p>You can order a <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test kit to be sent to your home or book an appointment at a walk-in or drive-through test site.</p>\n\n<p>If you’re getting a <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test because you’ve had a positive day 2 lateral flow travel test, you can only get the test sent to your home.</p>\n\n<div role=\"note\" aria-label=\"Warning\" class=\"application-notice help-notice\">\n<p>If you have symptoms, <a rel=\"external\" href=\"https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/self-isolation-and-treatment/when-to-self-isolate-and-what-to-do/\">self-isolate</a> straight away until you get your results. Do not leave home, except to post a test kit or for a PCR test appointment. <a rel=\"external\" href=\"https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/self-isolation-and-treatment/when-to-self-isolate-and-what-to-do/\">Check if the people you live with need to self-isolate on NHS.UK.</a></p>\n</div>\n\n<p>You should not use this service if you’ve received a positive <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test result in the last 90 days, unless you develop any new symptoms.</p>\n\n<h2 id=\"getting-a-pcr-test-if-you-have-no-symptoms\">Getting a <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test if you have no symptoms</h2>\n\n<p>You can also use this service if:</p>\n\n<ul>\n  <li>you’ve been in contact with someone who’s tested positive</li>\n  <li>you’ve been asked to get a test by a local council or someone from NHS Test and Trace</li>\n  <li>a GP or other health professional has asked you to get a test</li>\n  <li>you’re taking part in a government pilot project</li>\n  <li>you’ve been asked to get a test to confirm a positive result</li>\n  <li>you’ve received an unclear result and were told to get a second test</li>\n  <li>you need to get a test for someone you live with who has symptoms</li>\n  <li>you’re in the National Tactical Response Group</li>\n</ul>\n\n<h2 id=\"get-a-free-pcr-test\">Get a free <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test</h2>\n\n<p><a class=\"gem-c-button govuk-button govuk-button--start\" role=\"button\" href=\"https://self-referral.test-for-coronavirus.service.gov.uk/antigen\"> Start now <svg class=\"govuk-button__start-icon govuk-!-display-none-print\" xmlns=\"http://www.w3.org/2000/svg\" width=\"17.5\" height=\"19\" viewbox=\"0 0 33 40\" role=\"presentation\" focusable=\"false\"><path fill=\"currentColor\" d=\"M0 0h13l20 20-20 20H0l20-20z\"></path></svg></a></p>\n\n<h2 id=\"getting-a-rapid-lateral-flow-test-if-you-have-no-symptoms\">Getting a rapid lateral flow test if you have no symptoms</h2>\n\n<p>If you have no symptoms and are not eligible for a <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test for other reasons, you can <a href=\"https://www.gov.uk/order-coronavirus-rapid-lateral-flow-tests\">order rapid lateral flow tests to be sent to your home</a>.</p>\n\n<p>If you’re not sure about different test types, you can <a rel=\"external\" href=\"https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/testing/get-tested-for-coronavirus/\">find out which COVID-19 test you should get</a>.</p>\n\n<h2 id=\"if-youre-going-into-hospital\">If you’re going into hospital</h2>\n\n<p>You may need to get tested if you’re due to have surgery or a procedure.</p>\n\n<p>The hospital will arrange this for you. Contact your hospital department if you have any questions. ​</p>\n\n<h2 id=\"getting-tested-for-travel\">Getting tested for travel</h2>\n\n<p>You cannot use this service to book tests for travel.</p>\n\n<p>Find out what you need to do to:</p>\n\n<ul>\n  <li><a href=\"https://www.gov.uk/guidance/travel-abroad-from-england-during-coronavirus-covid-19\">travel abroad from England and return</a></li>\n  <li><a href=\"https://www.gov.uk/guidance/travel-to-england-from-another-country-during-coronavirus-covid-19\">travel to England if you live in another country</a></li>\n</ul>\n\n<h2 id=\"get-tested-as-soon-as-possible-if-you-have-symptoms\">Get tested as soon as possible if you have symptoms</h2>\n\n<p>Book a visit to a test site to have the test today. Test sites are open 7 days a week. Order a home test kit if you cannot get to a test site.</p>\n\n<p>You need to get the test done in the first 8 days of having symptoms.</p>\n\n<p>On days 1 to 7, you can get tested at a site or at home. If you’re ordering a home test kit on day 7, do it by 3pm.</p>\n\n<p>On day 8, you need to go to a test site - it’s too late to order a home test kit.</p>\n\n<h2 id=\"order-or-book-a-test-by-phone\">Order or book a test by phone</h2>\n\n<p>If you have problems or cannot use the online service, call 119.</p>\n\n<p>Lines are open 7am to 11pm.</p>\n\n<div role=\"note\" aria-label=\"Information\" class=\"application-notice info-notice\">\n<p>If no tests are available online, do not call helplines to get a test. No extra tests are available through the helplines.</p>\n</div>\n\n<h2 id=\"what-the-pcr-test-involves\">What the <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test involves</h2>\n\n<p>The test involves taking a swab of the inside of your nose and the back of your throat, using a long cotton bud.</p>\n\n<p>You can do the swab yourself (if you are aged 12 or over) or someone can do it for you. Parents or guardians have to swab test children aged 11 or under.</p>\n\n<h2 id=\"getting-a-pcr-test-for-someone-else\">Getting a <abbr title=\"Polymerase chain reaction\">PCR</abbr> test for someone else</h2>\n\n<p>If other people you live with have symptoms, you can order tests for up to 3 of them.</p>\n\n<p>If you’re applying for someone who’s 13 or over, check that they’re happy for you to get a test for them.</p>\n\n<h2 id=\"if-you-need-medical-advice-about-your-symptoms\">If you need medical advice about your symptoms</h2>\n\n<p>Get help at:</p>\n\n<ul>\n  <li><a rel=\"external\"
